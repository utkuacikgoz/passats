Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

When you use PassATS, the only data we process is:

  • Your uploaded resume file (PDF, DOC, or DOCX)
  • Payment information handled entirely by Stripe

What happens to your resume

Your resume is processed in real time, analyzed by our AI engine, and immediately discarded. We do not store, cache, or retain your file after analysis. We do not use your resume to train AI models.

Payment data

All payments are processed through Stripe. We never see or store your credit card details. Stripe's privacy policy governs their handling of your payment data.

Cookies & tracking

PassATS does not use cookies, analytics trackers, or third-party scripts. We do not track you across the web.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone. Your resume text is sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) for analysis and is subject to their data processing terms, which prohibit training on API inputs.
